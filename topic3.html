Introduction

Basketball is a fast-paced team sport played between two teams of five players. The objective is to score points by shooting the ball through the opponent's hoop. It requires speed, coordination, and teamwork.

History of Basketball

Basketball was invented in 1891 by Dr. James Naismith in the United States. It quickly became popular in schools and colleges. Today, basketball is one of the most followed sports globally.

Rules of Basketball

  1. Played in four quarters
  2. Each team has 5 players on the court
  3. Points are scored through field goals and free throws
  4. Dribbling is mandatory while moving with the ball
