Introduction

Football is the most popular sport in the world. It is played between two teams of eleven players each. The main objective of the game is to score goals by kicking the ball into the opponent's net. Football is known for its speed, skill, and global popularity.

History of Football

Modern football began in England in the 19th century. FIFA (Fédération Internationale de Football Association) was formed in 1904 to govern international football. The FIFA World Cup is now the most-watched sporting event in the world.

Rules of Football

  1. Match duration: 90 minutes
  2. Two halves of 45 minutes
  3. The team scoring more goals wins
  4. Offside rule applies
  5. Yellow and red cards for fouls
